Introduction

On this project the main objective is to create a Credit Model that gives three posible scores (Poor, Standard or Good) to a list of different people contained on the train.csv database file. This file it already includes a score for each case and the goal is to achieve the highest accuracy possible to this given model. The database has 100,000 rows (each row represents a person with all kind of information). To make the model firs is necessary to select the information it matters based on the FICO model.

The FICO model is a punctuation system that goes from 300 to 850 points, it was created on the 1950s and in 1989 the first version was released, since that year they been the most used system in the United States and a lot of other countries. Before anything else lets first see the database in order to select the variables we will need.

Model

The variables that we will pick are going to be the ones that are part of the five pillars of the FICO model. This pillars are:

  1. Payment history
  2. Amount debt
  3. Credit history
  4. New credit requests
  5. Types of credit

So now we have a little table that contains the variables selected and the pillar they are part of, including also the percentage it will have on the final score.

FICO Own model % of final score
Payment history Delay_from_due_date & Num_of_Delayed_Payment 35%
Amount debt Outstanding_Debt 30%
Credit history Credit_History_Age 10%
New credit requests Credit_Mix 15%
Types of credit Num_Credit_Inquiries 10%

The model is based on punctuation system that goes from 175 to 1000. The final score of every person is:

  • Poor: if the total of points it gets is under 600.
  • Standard: this score has to get minimum of 600 points and less than 800.
  • Good: to achieve this score is necessary to get 800 points or more.

Conclusions

The model has a good accuracy considering that is based on five pillars of a traditional model that is based on the past information and doesnt consider anything of machine learning or something similar. Maybe to upgrade the accuracy it could be considered other information as the salary or the credit ratio utilization to optimize the score and maybe improve the score of the people they have more salary and use more credit and still pay on time.
